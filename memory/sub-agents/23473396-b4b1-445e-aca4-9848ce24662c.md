#### Metagame Context: Underworld Breach Ban

**Ban Date:** March 31, 2025

**Reason for Ban:**
- Mox Opal unbanning put Breach into overdrive
- Absurd win rate among skilled pilots
- RCs had 20-30% Breach representation
- Frequently multiple Top 8 slots
- Can kill Turn 2, fights through hate exceptionally well
- Players dedicating 50%+ of sideboard to combat it

**Pre-Ban Dominance:**
- Temur Breach: 58.2% win rate at Regional Championships
- Four-Color Breach: 47.6% win rate
- US RC: 16 of Top 32 playing Temur Breach
- Alex Rohan won European RC with Temur Breach (1000 competitors)
- Jackson Knorr reached US RC finals with Temur Breach

**Post-Ban Impact:**
- Format opened up significantly
- Sideboards freed up from anti-Breach cards
- Wider range of strategies viable
- Boros Energy emerged as deck to beat
- Combo decks like Tameshi Belcher and Ruby Storm gained traction
- Goryo's Vengeance returned to metagame

#### Top Deck Archetypes (October-December 2025)

**Tier 1 Decks:**

1. **Boros Energy** (12.41% meta share - Aetherhub 90-day data)
   - Deck to beat post-Breach ban
   - Not as oppressive as pre-ban but still dominant
   - Struggled against Temur Breach when it was legal

2. **Izzet Affinity** (7.94% meta share)
   - Winner of MTG China Open Beijing (Yang Yi)
   - Benefits from Mox Opal unbanning
   - Key cards: Pinnacle Emissary, Weapons Manufacturing
   - Fast, explosive artifact strategy
   - Top performing in fall 2025

3. **Izzet Prowess** (8.84% meta share)
   - Tier 1 ranked at 9%
   - 2nd most played deck in Modern
   - Strong win rate at Pro Tour
   - Key card: Cori-Steel Cutter
   - All cards cost 2 or less, many free spells
   - Can grind with Expressive Iteration

4. **Amulet Titan** (7.37% meta share)
   - Most complex deck in format
   - Very powerful with experienced pilot
   - Established as top contender at Regional Championships

5. **Jeskai Blink/Jeskai Phelia** (8.19% meta share)
   - Won Champions Cup Yokohama (Hori Yuta)
   - Won MTGO Challenge Nov 9 (Ivan_Draw_Go)
   - Strong performance across multiple tournaments

6. **Domain Zoo/Domain Aggro** (6.04% meta share, also listed as 7.48% for 5c Domain)
   - Won MTGO Challenge Oct 16 (simoneromarm)
   - Key combo: Leyline of the Guildpact + Scion of Draco
   - Flying, lifelinking, hexproof threat
   - Cheap large creatures with burn and counters

**Tier 1.5-2 Decks:**

7. **Eldrazi Ramp** (2.52% meta share)
